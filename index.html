FAQ

Questions & answers

Is Quon free?
Yes — Quon is completely free. No signup, no payment, unlimited generations and downloads.
What kinds of QR codes can I make?
URL, plain text, contact (vCard), email, phone, and Wi-Fi — six types that cover business cards, Wi-Fi sharing, link sharing, and more.
Can I add a logo?
Yes. You can place a logo image in the center and adjust its size. Error-correction level is managed automatically to keep scans reliable.
Where is my data stored?
All QR codes are generated in your browser and never sent to any server. Recent history lives only in your localStorage.
Which file formats can I download?
PNG (raster) or SVG (vector). SVG is best for print; PNG is best for web and apps.
Can I use Quon commercially?
Yes. The QR codes you generate are yours — use them on business cards, marketing collateral, signage, packaging, anywhere.
